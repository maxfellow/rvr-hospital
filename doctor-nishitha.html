About

The specialist
behind your care.

Dr. Nishitha Shetty is a highly accomplished medical oncologist trained at Tata Memorial Centre, Mumbai — India's most prestigious cancer institution. With over 61 published research papers and active participation in international clinical trials across lung, breast, gastrointestinal and haematological malignancies, she brings a standard of oncology expertise to Mangalore that has previously been accessible only in major metro cities. Her practice is evidence-based, patient-centred and shaped by direct experience at the forefront of cancer research.
