About

The specialist
behind your care.

Dr. Keerthiraj B is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Radiodiagnosis and Imaging at KMC Mangalore. He teaches undergraduate and postgraduate students and serves as Consultant Neuro and Interventional Radiologist at Kasturba Hospital, Mangalore. With advanced training from SCTIMST, Trivandrum and Nizam's Institute of Medical Sciences, Hyderabad, he specializes in neuroradiology and interventional radiology procedures.

Areas of Expertise

Clinical specialisations

01
Neuroradiology
Advanced neuroimaging including CT, MRI, DSA, CTA, MRA for diagnosis of stroke, brain tumours, vascular malformations and spinal disorders.
02
Interventional Radiology
Minimally invasive image-guided procedures including endovascular interventions, embolization and vascular access procedures.
03
Neuroimaging
Specialized imaging of the brain, spine and head & neck region using advanced MRI and CT techniques for accurate diagnosis.
04
Radiodiagnosis
Comprehensive diagnostic imaging interpretation across all modalities including ultrasound, CT, MRI and conventional radiography.
05
UG & PG Teaching
Teaching radiodiagnosis to undergraduate MBBS students and postgraduate MD residents in the Department of Radiodiagnosis and Imaging.
06
Susceptibility Weighted Imaging
Research in susceptibility weighted MRI applications in neurological diagnosis including schizophrenia and related psychoses.
